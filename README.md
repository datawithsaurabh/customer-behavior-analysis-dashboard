## 🚀 Project Overview

The Customer Behavior Analysis Dashboard is an end-to-end Data Analytics project developed using SQL, Python, and Power BI. The project focuses on analyzing customer purchasing behavior, subscription trends, product performance, and revenue contribution to generate actionable business insights.

This project demonstrates the complete analytics workflow including data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), SQL-based business analysis, and interactive dashboard development.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze customer purchasing patterns.
- Understand subscription behavior.
- Identify top-performing products and categories.
- Measure discount effectiveness.
- Segment customers based on purchase history.
- Generate actionable business insights using interactive dashboards.

## 🔍 Key Business Questions Solved

### Revenue Analysis
- Revenue generated by Male vs Female customers
- Revenue contribution by Age Group
- Subscriber vs Non-Subscriber spending behavior

### Customer Analysis
- Customer Segmentation (New, Returning, Loyal)
- Repeat Buyer Analysis
- Subscription Trend Analysis

### Product Analysis
- Top Rated Products
- Top Selling Products by Category
- Discount Impact Analysis

### Shipping Analysis
- Purchase comparison between Standard and Express Shipping

---

## 📈 Key Insights

✅ Clothing category generated the highest revenue.

✅ Young Adult customers contributed the highest sales.

✅ Non-subscribers represented the majority of customers.

✅ Subscribed customers showed higher average spending.

✅ Discount campaigns positively impacted purchase frequency.

✅ Several products consistently maintained high review ratings.
